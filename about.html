About

This platform is developed by Momchil Petkov, student in genomics at the University of Bologna and silver medalist of the 2024 International Biology Olympiad.

My mission is to create intuitive, accessible tools and calculators that simplify genetic and genomic analysis for students, researchers, and enthusiasts alike. I believe that powerful bioinformatics resources shouldn't require advanced programming skills to use. By designing clean interfaces and providing meaningful outputs, I aim to make complex biological data easier to understand and explore. Ultimately, I want to support learning, discovery, and collaboration within the genetics community.
